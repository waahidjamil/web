Who We Are

Family isn't just our name. It's how we operate.

Jamil Realty was founded on a simple idea: real estate should be built on trust, not transactions. Growing up together, we learned that loyalty, hard work, and showing up for people aren't just values — they're non-negotiables.

Over 15 years and $500M+ in sales later, that foundation hasn't changed. We've built a team and a system that delivers results across Virginia, Maryland, DC, and West Virginia — but the approach is still personal, still hands-on, still family.

We don't just sell homes. We build relationships that last well beyond the closing table.
