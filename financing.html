What We Offer

Advanced Financing Strategies

In a shifting market, the interest rate is only one variable. We use advanced loan structures to lower your monthly payment and increase your purchasing power.

The "Cash-Like" Approval

We provide a Fully Underwritten Pre-Approval — an underwriter has already signed off on your income and assets, allowing us to waive financing contingencies and close in as little as 14 days. Your offer performs like cash.

2-1 Rate Buydowns

We structure a Temporary Rate Buydown, paid for by seller concessions we negotiate. This lowers your interest rate by 2% in year one and 1% in year two — saving hundreds per month while you settle in.

VA Loan Expertise

We specialize in 0% Down VA Loans with no mortgage insurance. We know how to navigate VA appraisals and residual income requirements to get our military clients to the closing table.

Jumbo & High-Balance

For homes exceeding conforming limits, we offer competitive Jumbo Loan products with access to portfolio lenders who offer flexibility on debt-to-income ratios for high-net-worth individuals and business owners.
