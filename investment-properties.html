The Investment Thesis

Why Northern Virginia Outperforms.

The DMV corridor isn't just government jobs anymore. Amazon HQ2, the data center boom, and Virginia's tech corridor have created a supply-demand imbalance that savvy investors are capitalizing on.

Consistent Appreciation

NoVA averaged 5.8% annual appreciation over the past decade — 2x the national average.

Recession-Resistant Demand

Federal employment, defense contractors, and Big Tech create a diversified tenant base.

Infrastructure Catalysts

Silver Line Metro, Amazon HQ2, and the Dulles data center corridor drive outsized returns.
